On The Road

What this journey is exactly, will probably only become clear over time.


Living in a van is more intensely attending to the very basic daily things: eating, drinking and finding a place for the night. Sometimes I might encounter beautiful places along the road, but more impotant than looking for them is going slower, looking around, outwards and inwards.


The goal of this blog is to have a general place for me to refer to. Sometimes I will share some pictures, somtimes reflections on what this travelling existence brings. The goal is to give an update approximately once a week.
